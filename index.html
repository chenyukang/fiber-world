How Does Fiber Network Work?

1 of 4 Off-Chain Payment Channels

An off-chain payment channel allows two parties to lock funds on-chain, conduct unlimited instant transactions off-chain, and settle the final balance on-chain when the channel is closed.

2 of 4 Multi-Hop Routing

Multi-hop routing enables payments between parties who don't have direct channels by routing through intermediate nodes in the network.

3 of 4 Cross-Chain Interoperability

Fiber Network enables seamless interoperability with Bitcoin Lightning Network for cross-chain payments and asset transfers.

4 of 4 Multi-Asset Support

Support for various digital assets including CKB and RGB++ tokens, providing users with diverse payment options.
